BE AWARE that corrupting an item can turn it into a rare.
All items have a 25% chance to be turned into a rare (brick) upon corrupting.
The chance of getting a successful corruption (bonus) is 75% for equipment.
Charms have 25% chance to be turned into a rare, 50% chance to get a bonus and 25% chance to get nothing.
Certain items cannot be corrupted to avoid abuse or too powerful items (e.g. Damage Augmenter, Torch).
Items can still be modified by other recipes, the corrupted tag only prevents them from being corrupted again.
After transmuting an item with a Worldstone Shard, you need to transmute it again to effectively corrupt it.
